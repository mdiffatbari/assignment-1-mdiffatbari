Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has a rich history of innovation, from ancient weaving techniques to modern-day technological advancements.

Sustainability

Sustainability is about meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

Infrastructure

In Bangladesh, significant strides have been made in recent years to improve infrastructure, with a focus on transportation, energy, and telecommunications.

Empowerment

In the context of Bangladesh, empowerment is a critical factor in driving social progress and achieving sustainable development.
